# - Gyártásautomatizálás
This page contains the subject materials for Manufacturing Automation, including production-system modeling, industrial control concepts, and supporting engineering notes. The subject is offered in Hungarian and emphasizes automation architectures used in modern manufacturing environments.

Themes
- **Introduction**: Foundations of manufacturing automation and industrial production systems.
- **Automation architectures**: Layers of industrial control from field devices to supervisory systems.
- **Sensors and actuators in production**: Device classes, integration patterns, and engineering tradeoffs.
- **PLC and control logic**: Basic programmable control concepts and automation-oriented logic design.
- **Robotized manufacturing cells**: Robotics within automated production and assembly environments.
- **Industrial communication**: Fieldbus, Ethernet-based industrial communication, and connected system integration.
- **Cyber-physical production systems**: Linking software, hardware, sensing, and operational processes.
- **Simulation and digital modeling**: Modeling production flows and validating automation concepts before deployment.
- **Safety, testing, and validation**: Reliability, system verification, and practical evaluation concerns in industrial automation.
