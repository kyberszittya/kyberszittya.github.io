# - Ipar 4.0 (Industry 4.0 applications)
This page contains the full subject materials for Industry 4.0, including course-level topics, engineering workflows, and supporting notes. The subject is offered in Hungarian, covering a range of topics relevant to Industry 4.0 in the context of autonomous systems.

Official course site: [sze-ipar40-page.vercel.app](https://sze-ipar40-page.vercel.app/)

Themes
- **Introduction**: Overview of Industry 4.0.
- **Introduction to NIST Cyber-physical framework**: Understanding the NIST cyber-physical framework and modeling.
- **Systems engineering**: Principles and practices of systems engineering in the context of Industry 4.0.
- **System modeling**: Using SysML for system modeling in the context of Industry 4.0.
- **Digital twins**: Understanding digital twins and their applications in Industry 4.0.
- **Communication patterns and protocols**: Exploring communication patterns and protocols used in Industry 4.0, including Pub/Sub, RPC, parameter server.
- **Distributed systems**: Principles and practices of distributed systems in the context of Industry 4.0. Technologies including MQTT, OPC-UA, and ROS.
- **Simulation**: Techniques for simulating Industry 4.0 systems.
- **Measurement and testing**: Methods for measuring and testing Industry 4.0 systems, including performance metrics and validation techniques.
- **Security and privacy**: Understanding security and privacy considerations in Industry 4.0 systems, including threat modeling and mitigation strategies.
- **Robotics**: Introduction to robotics in the context of Industry 4.0, including robotic systems, control, and applications.
- **Wireless communication**: Exploring wireless communication technologies used in Industry 4.0, including 5G and Wi-Fi 6, low-power wide-area networks (LPWANs), and their applications in industrial environments.
